Questions & answers

How and when can I get access?

Notion AI is launching in alpha on November 16th. Access will be granted on a first-come-first-served basis to those who join the waitlist.

Why is there a waitlist?

We’re rolling out Notion AI slowly to ensure that the quality of AI-generated content remains high, and to get early feedback from you to inform future features. We’ll open the feature to more people as our AI scales and improves.

Will Notion AI be enabled in my workspace by default?

No. Currently, only users who join the waitlist will be eligible to get access once the alpha is open. A workspace owner will also have to turn the feature on before it is enabled for workspace members.

Will Notion AI cost extra?

Notion AI will be free in alpha, but will likely cost extra in the future.

How will my data be used?

Your data is still encrypted and private following our standard data protection practices.

Our AI features are available on an opt-in basis. If you choose to enable Notion AI, any information used to power Notion AI will be shared with our partners for the sole purpose of providing you with the Notion AI features. We do not allow any partners or 3rd parties to use your data for training their models or any other purpose.

We will not use your data to train our models unless you give us express permission to do so.

For information specific to Notion AI, see the Notion AI Program Terms.

Where can I learn more about Notion’s privacy practices?

For information specific to Notion AI, please see the Notion AI Program Terms.

For general information about Notion’s security practices, you can visit notion.so/security to learn more about how we protect your data. Our terms and privacy agreement also goes into further detail.
